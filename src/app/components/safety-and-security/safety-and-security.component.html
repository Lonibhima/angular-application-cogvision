Vision based Poka-yoke

What is Poka-yoke?
Mistake proofing/ Fool proofing, or its Japanese equivalent poka-yoke (pronounced PO-ka yo-KAY), is the use of any automatic device or method that either makes it impossible for an error to occur or makes the error immediately obvious once it has occurred. It is a common process analysis tool.
Cogvision has built an AI framework, which will facilitate poka-yoke in Industrial inspection by helping manufacturing companies drive towards zero defects. Additionally, to reduce overall production costs associated due to delay or repeated processes due to human errors which can be controlled effectively using Cogvision’s Poka Yoke AI framework.
It also helps management at all levels to streamline the manufacturing process.
